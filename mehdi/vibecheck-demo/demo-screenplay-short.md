## Shortened 3-Question Demo Screenplay

**Visual:** the AI interviewer opens a fresh product review session.

**AI Interviewer:**
“Okay, let’s start simple. What is VibeCheck actually helping with in practice?”

**Narrator / Voiceover:**
“It helps people make sense of messy evidence. Instead of forcing a quick yes or no from a deck alone, it keeps the decision grounded in what is known, what is missing, and what still needs to be checked.”

**AI Interviewer:**
“Right. And when you’re looking at a founder or a team, how does it avoid sounding confident when the evidence is still shaky?”

**Narrator / Voiceover:**
“It keeps the source trail attached to each claim. So the reviewer can see what supports something, what conflicts with it, and what is still unknown instead of getting a polished summary that hides the uncertainty.”

**AI Interviewer:**
“Last thing — once it has done that, what still has to come from the investor?”

**Narrator / Voiceover:**
“The investor still has to make the call. VibeCheck can organize the evidence and point out the gaps, but the final judgment stays with the person using it.”
