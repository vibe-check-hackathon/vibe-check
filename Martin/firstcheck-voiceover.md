# FirstCheck Voiceover

FirstCheck is a simple venture screening tool built to find the most promising founders before anyone else. We also have an outbound engine that scans GitHub, patents, launches, and public signals to find strong teams early. For simplicity, this demo shows the inbound path.

A founder submits a company name, pitch deck, and basic team information. That is enough for FirstCheck to start screening.

The application appears instantly in the investor dashboard. The system parses the deck, enriches public data, and creates one profile per cofounder, including early personality hypotheses.

If the team and idea look promising, FirstCheck automatically sends an invitation to an AI interview.

In the interview, founders talk to an AI agent. As they answer, FirstCheck tests its hypotheses live, updates confidence, and feeds those insights into the deal and negotiation model.

At the end, the human investor stays in the loop. Carl-Philipp Beichert reviews the evidence-backed recommendation and approves the deal.

Once approved, the term sheet lands directly in the founder’s inbox.

FirstCheck takes founders from first signal to investment decision in 24 hours.
