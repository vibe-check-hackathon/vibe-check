## 60-Second System Architecture Presentation

### Slide 1 — One Living Investment System

**Time:** 0–7 seconds

**Slide content**

- Three-stage pipeline: **Source → Develop → Match**
- Opportunity Card shown as the central record connecting all stages
- Inputs: decks, forms, profiles, social media, GitHub, references

**Speaker script**\
“Our architecture turns fragmented founder and company information into a continuous investment workflow. At its center is the Opportunity Card: a living record that connects sourcing, assessment, interviews, negotiation, and the final decision.”

**Message to land**\
The system is not a collection of disconnected agents. The Opportunity Card provides shared state across the entire investment lifecycle.

---

### Slide 2 — Source: AI Builds the Opportunity Card

**Time:** 7–17 seconds

**Slide content**

- Intake and sourcing agents
- Example inputs: pitch deck, CV, LinkedIn, GitHub
- Example output: Opportunity Card containing founder and idea facts
- Source links and trust levels attached to material claims

**Speaker script**\
“In sourcing, AI agents process decks and intake forms, browse public sources such as LinkedIn, social media, and GitHub, and create thousands of Opportunity Cards. The memory and evidence store deduplicates sources, preserves founder history, and links important claims to evidence and trust levels.”

**Message to land**\
AI converts scattered information into structured, traceable investment opportunities at scale.

---

### Slide 3 — Develop: Agentic Research Applies the Thesis

**Time:** 17–28 seconds

**Slide content**

- Research agents operating in a loop
- Inputs: investment thesis, historical data, references, background checks
- Separate assessment areas: founder, market, idea-versus-market
- Artifact example: enriched Opportunity Card with risks and negotiation notes

**Speaker script**\
“In development, specialized research agents repeatedly apply the fund’s investment thesis. They compare markets and companies, run reference and background checks, and assess the founder, market, and idea-versus-market independently. Their findings enrich the card and prioritize which opportunities deserve attention.”

**Message to land**\
AI does more than summarize: it applies investment logic iteratively while preserving distinct dimensions of judgment.

---

### Slide 4 — Match: Live AI Interviews Resolve Uncertainty

**Time:** 28–38 seconds

**Slide content**

- Vibe-check and interview agents

- Live founder and team interaction

- Multimodal signals:
  - Personality evaluation
  - Facial-expression analysis
  - Tone and sentiment analysis

- Opportunity Card updated during the interview

**Speaker script**\
“In matching, AI interview agents conduct live founder conversations and update the Opportunity Card as new evidence emerges. The vibe check combines social-science-based personality evaluation, computer vision for facial expression, and NLP for tone and sentiment to assess founder-founder and founder-investor fit.”

**Message to land**\
The interview is an active evidence-gathering step, not a static questionnaire.

---

### Slide 5 — From Evidence to Negotiable Terms

**Time:** 38–50 seconds

**Slide content**

- Negotiation and term-sheet agent

- BATNA-based negotiation model

- Artifact progression:
  - Negotiation notes
  - Annotated draft term sheet
  - Final term sheet or decline

- Example annotation linking a proposed term to supporting evidence

**Speaker script**\
“The negotiation agent converts approved recommendations into an annotated draft term sheet. Using a BATNA-based model, it explains the evidence behind proposed terms and supports honest negotiation. Founder responses and negotiation notes then drive the final decision to issue terms or decline.”

**Message to land**\
Terms emerge from the accumulated evidence and negotiation model, rather than appearing as unexplained numbers.

---

### Slide 6 — Human-Controlled Opportunity OS

**Time:** 50–60 seconds

**Slide content**

- Component map around the Opportunity Card:
  - Sourcing agents
  - Evidence and memory store
  - Research agents
  - Interview agents
  - Negotiation agent
  - Investor workspace

- Human investor shown with final approve/veto control

**Speaker script**\
“Together, these components form an Opportunity OS. The investor workspace surfaces recommendations, unresolved risks, and the evidence behind every conclusion. AI compresses sourcing, diligence, interviews, and negotiation, but the human investor retains final approval or veto authority.”

**Message to land**\
The architecture combines AI scale and continuous evidence gathering with transparent human control.
