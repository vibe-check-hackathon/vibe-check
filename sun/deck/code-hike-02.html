Opportunity OS — Signal → Deal

Three agents carry one Opportunity Card from raw signal to signed deal. Each stage is owned by a single agent; every fact, assessment, and term flows through the Card. Humans keep final authority.

Scroll down to build the Card. Scroll up to rewind it.

▼ SOURCING · RESEARCHING · INTERVIEWING
Stage 01 / Fig. 1 — Funnel

01Sourcing

agent: Sourcer — gathers facts into the Opportunity Card

Every inbound deck, form, and public signal is parsed into verifiable facts. Founders are recognized across opportunities, and each fact lands in the Card with its source attached.

Tech stack

Next.js / Temporal / LlamaParse / Zod / Drizzle ORM / PostgreSQL (RLS) / S3

Theory

  • Entity resolution & fact provenance
  • Evidence-cited knowledge capture
Stage 02 / Fig. 2 — Lenses

02Researching

agent: Researcher — evaluates the Opportunity against the investment thesis

The Card is judged along three independent axes — Founder, Market, Idea × Market. Evidence is gathered per axis; contradictions stay visible. The result is a draft term sheet where every number traces back to evidence.

Tech stack

Temporal / OpenAI Agents SDK / pgvector / TypeScript calculation engine

Theory

  • Investment thesis modeling
  • Zero-knowledge proofs for due-diligence sourcing
Stage 03 / Fig. 3 — Convergence

03Interviewing

agent: Interviewer — negotiates towards the optimal deal

A live voice interview resolves the Card's open questions, then negotiation converges founder and investor positions within human-approved bounds. Anything out of bounds escalates to the investor, who keeps final authority.

Tech stack

ElevenLabs Agents / WebRTC / Server-Sent Events / Clerk / Temporal

Theory

  • Multi-user social / personality modeling
  • Negotiation theory (BATNA, Nash bargaining)
