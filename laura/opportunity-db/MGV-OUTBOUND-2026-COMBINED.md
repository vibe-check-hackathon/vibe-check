# MGV Outbound Founder & Startup Intelligence — January to July 2026

Prospective outbound cards built from public sources for **January, February, March, May,
June and July 2026**. The set intentionally contains both sourcing directions:

1. **Founder/technical signal first** — a hackathon win, public engineering release or visible
   technical execution creates the lead.
2. **Company first** — a funding, accelerator or customer signal identifies an existing team,
   which is then evaluated through the same evidence framework.

The card structure is a compact implementation of the uploaded Outbound Person & Potential
Team Intelligence Brief: identity, company hypothesis, stage, evidence, scoring, gaps, outbound
strategy and final disposition. It also follows the existing OPP-MGV card convention.

## Integrity and use rules

- Public and permitted professional information only; no private contact data is included.
- Every material statement is either sourced, explicitly marked `claimed`, or recorded as a gap.
- The **Founder Score is evidence coverage and strength**, not a personality or moral score.
- Protected characteristics, private life, inferred health, political views and other sensitive data
  are excluded.
- A high score advances a candidate to human diligence; it does not automatically deploy capital.
- Funding announcements are retrospective triggers. Always verify whether allocation remains.
- Outbound must be personalized but non-surveillance-like, with opt-out and suppression controls.

Unknown evidence receives no points until verified. Scores should be recalculated whenever a new
milestone, customer reference, technical artifact, financing event or contradictory fact appears.
